About Me!

Hi! My name is Hana Khouli, I am 21 years old and I live in Everett, WA. I go to Edmonds Community College and I am studying Graphic Design. I am studying Graphic design because I already enjoy anything that is art related and I thought that I should end up pursuing a job that relates to art or something that would allow me to be creative. I thought that Graphic Design is a way for me to take my love got being creative to another level.

A little bit more information about me, is that I grew up in Dubai, UAE. I have three brothers (two older and one younger than me) and I have a pet German Shepherd, named Laika. My Greman Shepherd was named after the first Russian dog that ever went to space. Unfortunately, that dog did not make it back home alive.

On a lighter note, out of my whole family I am the only one who is really good at anything that is art related and it is really easy for me to learn a new art skill. I really enjoyed Learning how to code in this class and I am really excited for my potential future in Graphic Design and where it could lead me to the next chapter in life.
